About Virtual Labs

An Initiative of Ministry of Education Under the National Mission on Education through ICT

Empowering students with hands-on experience in a virtual environment.

Our Mission

The Virtual Labs project aims to bridge the gap in laboratory education by providing remote access to high-quality lab experiments for students, researchers, and educators. Our mission is to enable experiential learning in science and engineering through interactive, web-enabled experiments, ensuring that every learner, regardless of location or infrastructure limitations, can gain practical exposure to complex concepts.

Our Vision

We envision a future where technology-driven education removes barriers to hands-on learning. By leveraging ICT-based remote experimentation, we strive to make laboratory education universally accessible, fostering innovation, curiosity, and collaboration among students, researchers, and academic institutions. Our goal is to create an ecosystem that enhances learning experiences and supports the academic growth of future scientists and engineers.

Why Choose Us?

Over 100+ Virtual Labs with 700+ experiments covering various science and engineering disciplines. Inclusivity & Outreach – Beneficial for students without access to advanced lab facilities, high-school students exploring STEM, and researchers seeking collaborative experimentation. Government-Backed Initiative – Developed under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, ensuring quality, credibility, and alignment with academic standards. Enhancing Teaching & Learning – Faculty members and institutions can integrate these virtual labs into their curriculum, enriching their teaching methodologies with cutting-edge educational tools.
